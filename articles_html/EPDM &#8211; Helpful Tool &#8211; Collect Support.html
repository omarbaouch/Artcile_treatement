Sometimes the archive log file is not enough to troubleshoot your vault – the “Collect Support Information…” tool allows you to quickly generate a variety of logs and reports.

STEP 1: In the SolidWorks Enterprise PDM Administration Window, right-click on the vault and click on Collect Support Information…

STEP 2: Choose the information to be generated.

STEP 3: Add any files or folders from the Archive, if required.

 

STEP 4: If needed or requested, create a back-up of the SQL database.

 

STEP 5: Enter any additional information that you feel is important.

STEP 6: Create the package.

STEP 7: Send the resulting zip file to the support help desk of your local SolidWorks reseller.
