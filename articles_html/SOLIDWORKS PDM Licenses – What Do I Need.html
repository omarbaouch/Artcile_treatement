With SOLIDWORKS PDM, engineers and designers no longer accidentally (or purposefully) step on each others toes while working on a file. With defined workflows, the right person at the right time has access to the file. But how do decide what SOLIDWORKS PDM license is best for you and your business?

Watch the on-demand video below as System Architect – Data Management, Patricia Bar, goes through each license type that SOLIDWORKS PDM offers in-depth. She will discuss the pros and cons of each, what the intended usage is and who needs to have licenses available at all times.
