As of
SOLIDWORKS 2018 SP2
,
SOLIDWORKS PDM
integration has been added to the Standalone version of
SOLIDWORKS Inspection
!
Implementing the new functionality is easy, first toggle the option ‘on’ in your SOLIDWORKS Inspection options as shown below:
SOLIDWORKS PDM Integration Add-In
Once you have this option toggled, a new tab/section dedicated to SOLIDWORKS PDM will appear in your ribbon as shown below.
SOLIDWORKS PDM Ribbon
All desired SOLIDWORKS PDM functionality is present! Including checking in/out your Inspection Project, access to previous versions, and state changes!
